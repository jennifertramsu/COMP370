Pedestrian, 72, hit by car in Pointe-Claire

Traffic was barred from St-Jean Blvd. between Brunswick and Salaberry Blvds. and on Labrosse Ave. between Auto Plaza Ave. and Revcon Ave.

Author of the article:
Montreal Gazette
Published Oct 25, 2023  •  Last updated 9 hours ago  •  1 minute read
Join the conversation

Article content

A 72-year-old woman was expected to survive Wednesday after she was struck by a car at an intersection in Pointe-Claire, causing severe injuries.

Montreal police said the incident occurred at 9:45 a.m. at the corner of St-Jean Blvd. and Labrosse Ave. The impact occurred as the car, headed west on Labrosse Ave., turned to head south on St-Jean Blvd.

Article content

The driver, a 78-year-old man, remained at the scene.

The pedestrian was brought to a hospital in critical condition, but a Montreal police spokesperson said later that her was not in danger.
